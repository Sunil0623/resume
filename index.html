Prediction of Autism using ML

Project Goals:

Enhance early detection of autism spectrum disorder in children under 3 years old using machine learning algorithms. The project aims to facilitate early intervention and treatment for positive cases.

Software Requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows/Mac
  • Frontend: Bootstrap
  • IDE: Spyder
  • Libraries: pandas, numpy, pickle, matplotlib, etc.

Hardware Requirements:

  • Hard Disk: 500 GB or more
  • RAM: 4GB

Modules Details:

  • Parents/Guardian:

    Parents or guardians can log in to the site and provide answers to behavioral questions on behalf of children requiring tests. Predictions are generated based on the responses provided.

  • Doctor:

    Medical professionals conduct assessments based on the child's behavior and observations made during the treatment process.

Conclusion

A prediction model has been developed to accurately predict autism traits in children using the AQ-10 dataset. This model outperforms existing screening methods and a user-friendly application has been created for easy prediction of autism traits. This research offers an effective approach to detect autism traits early in children, potentially reducing delays in diagnosis and associated costs.
